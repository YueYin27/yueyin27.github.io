About Me

I am a first year PhD student in the School of Computing at the Australian National University, supervised by Dylan Campbell, Hongdong Li, and Stephen Gould. Currently, I am working on 3D Reconstruction and physically-plausible generation. Before that, I received a Master's degree in Machine Learning and Computer Vision from ANU in 2024, and also received a Bachelor's degree in Internet of Things Engineering from Jiangsu University of Science and Technology in 2022.

Research Interests

I am passionate about computer vision, particularly 3D Vision. I am also interested in image generation, 3D generation, and physically-plausible generation techniques.
