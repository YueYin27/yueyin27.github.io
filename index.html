About Me个人简介

I am a first-year PhD student in the School of Computing at The Australian National University, supervised by Dr. Dylan Campbell, Prof. Hongdong Li, and Prof. Stephen Gould. Currently, I am working on projects in 3D reconstruction and physically plausible generation. Before that, I received a master's degree in Machine Learning and Computer Vision from the Australian National University, and a bachelor's degree in Internet of Things Engineering from Jiangsu University of Science and Technology with the Excellent Graduate Award.

我是澳大利亚国立大学计算机学院的一年级博士生，导师是 Dylan Campbell 博士，Li Hongdong 教授和 Stephen Gould 教授。 我当前的研究方向是三维重建和符合物理规律的图像生成。 在此之前，我获得了澳大利亚国立大学的机器学习与计算机视觉硕士学位，以及江苏科技大学的物联网工程学士学位，并荣获"优秀毕业生"称号。

Research Interests研究方向

I am passionate about computer vision, particularly 3D vision, and have strong interests in image generation, 3D generation, and physically plausible generation techniques.

我热衷于计算机视觉，特别是三维视觉领域，并对图像生成、三维生成和符合物理规律的生成技术有浓厚兴趣。
